Your Enterprise Architecture Visible. Connected. Understood.

What is Waltz?

Waltz is the open source platform that turns fragmented architecture information into a living, connected map of applications, data flows, people, capabilities and change initiatives.

It helps enterprise architecture, engineering, portfolio and risk teams answer critical questions with evidence instead of spreadsheets.

Why We Built Waltz

Enterprise teams need to make high-impact technology decisions quickly, but architecture data is often fragmented, hard to trust, and hard to connect across systems. Waltz addresses these core pains.

Fragmented & Outdated Data

Critical architecture information lives in spreadsheets, disconnected tools, and tribal knowledge, making a reliable current-state view hard to maintain.

Slow, Cross-System Analysis

Answering simple questions about capabilities, applications, data flows, and ownership often requires stitching together evidence from many systems.

Audit & Regulatory Pressure

Regulatory and risk programmes like DORA, MiFID and Basel require accurate, explainable topology and lineage evidence, but many organisations struggle to produce it on demand.

Waltz Connects the Dots

Turn scattered architecture data into a shared operating model for change, governance and risk.
Open to the organisation, enriched by the people closest to the estate.

Living Architecture Map

Visualise your entire application estate and how every system connects, from logical data flows to physical implementations.

Org-to-App Compliance

Navigate from a business unit to its full technology footprint instantly — costs, capabilities, data sources and team ownership.

Data Lineage & Golden Sources

Track upstream and downstream data flows across your entire organisation. Identify authoritative data sources and flag non-compliant consumers.

Crowdsourced Accuracy

Empower every employee to contribute and correct architecture data. The best source of information about your landscape is the people who work in it.

Roadmaps & Future State

Document planned application changes, retirements, and replacements. Align your technology roadmap with your strategy.

Surveys & Assessments

Issue targeted questionnaires to application owners. Collect structured data for compliance programmes, rationalisation exercises, and audits.

Custom Report Grids

Build powerful aggregated views. Slice your technology estate by any taxonomy, org unit, or data type — no SQL required.

KPIs and Metrics

Define and track your own architecture metrics. Measure the proportion of applications with golden source data, or the percentage of applications in scope for a regulatory programme.

Integrates, Doesn't Replace

Waltz complements PowerDesigner, Archimate, Sparx and other EA tools. Aggregate data from your existing systems without ripping anything out.

Built for the Problems That Matter Most

Regulatory Compliance

Generate application topology reports required for DORA, MiFID II, BCBS 239 and other regulatory programmes in minutes, not weeks.

Application Rationalisation

Identify duplicated capabilities, legacy applications, and cost-saving consolidation opportunities across your entire estate.

Data Lineage & Governance

Track critical data flows from source to consumer. Define golden sources and measure compliance against them.

M&A and Transformation Programmes

Quickly understand the technology landscape of acquired entities. Model future-state architectures for major programmes.

Used by

Waltz is used by teams working through regulatory scope, application rationalisation, lineage and major transformation in complex enterprise environments.
