Your Enterprise Architecture Visible. Connected. Understood.

What is Waltz?

Waltz is the open source platform that gives your organisation a living, connected map of the technology landscape: applications, data flows, people, capabilities and change initiatives in one place.

It helps leaders, architects, engineering teams and risk functions align decisions with evidence.

Why Architecture Decisions Stall

Scattered & Stale

Architecture data lives in spreadsheets, disconnected tools and people's heads. By the time it's consolidated, it's already out of date.

Hard to Navigate

Finding how a business capability maps to applications, data flows and org units requires jumping between a dozen systems.

Compliance Risk

Regulatory programs like DORA, MiFID II and Basel require accurate application topology — but most firms can't produce it on demand.

Waltz Connects the Dots

A structured wiki for your architecture
open to everyone, enriched by everyone.

Living Architecture Map

Visualize your entire application estate and how every system connects, from logical data flows to physical implementations.

Org-to-App Compliance

Navigate from a business unit to its full technology footprint instantly — costs, capabilities, data sources and team ownership.

Data Lineage & Golden Sources

Track upstream and downstream data flows across your entire organisation. Identify authoritative data sources and flag non-compliant consumers.

Crowdsourced Accuracy

Empower every employee to contribute and correct architecture data. The best source of information about your landscape is the people who work in it.

Roadmaps & Future State

Document planned application changes, retirements, and replacements. Align your technology roadmap with your strategy.

Surveys & Assessments

Issue targeted questionnaires to application owners. Collect structured data for compliance programs, rationalization exercises, and audits.

Custom Report Grids

Build powerful aggregated views. Slice your technology estate by any taxonomy, org unit, or data type — no SQL required.

KPI's and Metrics

Define and track your own architecture metrics. Measure the proportion of applications with golden source data, or the percentage of applications in scope for a regulatory program.

Integrates, Doesn't Replace

Waltz complements PowerDesigner, Archimate, Sparx and other EA tools. Aggregate data from your existing systems without ripping anything out.
