A slight digression from the article series — a look at something in progress: using AI agents to analyse a Waltz repository and automatically create structured findings.

<!--more-->

The agents have different personas, so they look for different things, each with their own qualification criteria.

Why is this compelling? The agents can scan a large Waltz installation in a fraction of the time — minutes, not days or weeks. They surface areas that warrant attention to humans in the loop, who can then further qualify and triage.

A practical application of AI in an enterprise context. Best of all, you can plug in whichever model you like and set a token budget.
